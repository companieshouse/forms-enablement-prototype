Reducing your share capital (SH19)

This service is used to reduce your share capital for a private or public limited company.

It costs £10 to reduce your share capital, and you will need:

  • the company number
  • the company authentication code
  • a credit or debit card

You can also use the same day service, which costs £50.

It normally takes 10 minutes to complete this process.
