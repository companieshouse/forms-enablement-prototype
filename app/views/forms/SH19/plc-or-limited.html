Additional information

An administrative reduction in capital requires the following documents:

  • special resolution authorising the capital reduction
  • solvency statement
  • directors’ statement of compliance

You can file any of these documents separately but the Statement of Capital will not be processed until you’ve filed them all successfully. The directors’ statement needs to be an original document and therefore must be submitted on paper.
