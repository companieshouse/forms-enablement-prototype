Is your company a private or public limited company?

If you're filing for a private limited company, you'll need the following documents:

  • solvency statement
  • special resolution authorising the capital reduction
  • director's statement

If you're filing for a public limited company, you'll need the following documents:

  • order of court
  • special resolution authorising capital reduction
