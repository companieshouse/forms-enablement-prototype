Before you start

Getting ready to close your company

An application for closing a company can only be made if the company is eligble. For example, a company can not apply to close if in the last 3 months it has traded or otherwise carried on business.

Read the guidance on when a company cannot apply to close.

Who to tell about your company closing

Directors applying to close their company must tell all interested parties that the application has been made within 7 days. Interested parties may include:

  • members, usually shareholders
  • creditors
  • employees

For more information, read the guidance on who to tell about your company closing.

Warning It is an offence not to tell all interested parties
