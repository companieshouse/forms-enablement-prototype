Understanding the company's responsibilities

Who to tell about your company closing

A company must inform all interested parties that it is going to close. All interested parties must be informed within 7 days of the application being submitted.
Interested parties may include:

  • members, usually shareholders
  • creditors
  • employees

Read the full list of who to tell about your company closing.

Warning It is an offence not to tell all interested parties
